# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary users are shop admins who need a simple daily attendance control panel. Secondary users are employees who scan a QR code on their own phones and submit clock-in or clock-out records quickly before or after a shift.

## Product Purpose

EzyTime records employee clock-in and clock-out events from a QR scan, stores the logs in a free hosted backend, and gives the admin a daily summary with calculated working time. Success means staff can submit a record in under a minute and the admin can understand the day without exporting data or checking raw timestamps.

## Brand Personality

Cute, calm, practical. The interface should feel friendly for restaurant floor staff while staying clear and reliable for admin work.

## Anti-references

Avoid corporate HR software, spreadsheet-heavy dashboards, dark command-center styling, marketing landing pages, and decorative UI that slows down the core scan-and-submit workflow.

## Design Principles

1. Scan first, type little.
2. Make the current day obvious.
3. Keep admin controls calm and trustworthy.
4. Use friendly visual details only when they support the task.
5. Design for phones at the QR station and laptops at the admin desk.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, touch targets of at least 44px, reduced-motion support, clear Thai labels, and layouts that work on narrow mobile screens.
